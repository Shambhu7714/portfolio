About Me

I'm Shambhu Kumar, a recent M.Sc. graduate in Big Data Analytics from the Central University of Rajasthan. My passion for data science is fueled by a strong foundation in computer applications (B.C.A. from Patna University) and a year of honing my skills in Python, Machine Learning, NLP, and various Deep Learning frameworks.

My internship at BOT Shreyasi provided invaluable experience developing and maintaining robust RESTful APIs with Django, building secure user profiles, and tackling highly scalable projects. This experience not only sharpened my technical skills but also instilled critical thinking and problem-solving abilities for complex data challenges.

My projects, like Loan Prediction and House Price Prediction models, showcase my ability to apply theoretical knowledge for real-world impact. I'm eager to leverage my analytical skills, dedication, and passion for data to contribute meaningfully to a dynamic team while continuously learning and refining my expertise in data science.
